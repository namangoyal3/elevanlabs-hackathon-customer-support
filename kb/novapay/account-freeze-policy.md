# Account Freeze Policy

## Overview
A NovaPay account may be frozen temporarily or permanently due to compliance requirements, security concerns, or regulatory orders. A frozen account restricts all outgoing transactions while allowing the customer to view their balance and transaction history.

## Reasons an Account May Be Frozen

| Reason | Type | Duration |
|--------|------|----------|
| KYC expired or not completed | Compliance | Until KYC is completed |
| Suspicious transaction activity | Security | 24–72 hours (pending review) |
| Multiple failed login attempts | Security | 2–24 hours |
| Regulatory / court order | Legal | Until order is lifted |
| Customer-requested freeze | Customer | Until customer requests unfreeze |
| Chargeback fraud investigation | Fraud | Until investigation completes |

## What a Frozen Account Can and Cannot Do

| Action | Allowed |
|--------|---------|
| View balance | Yes |
| View transaction history | Yes |
| Receive incoming payments | Yes (funds held, not accessible) |
| Send payments / transfers | No |
| Top up wallet | No |
| Withdraw to bank | No |
| Apply for loan | No |

## How to Unfreeze an Account

### KYC-Related Freeze
1. Customer completes or re-does KYC via app
2. Account is unfrozen automatically within 2–4 hours after KYC approval
3. If KYC is taking too long, escalate to KYC team

### Security-Related Freeze
1. Customer contacts support to verify identity
2. Agent verifies via OTP on registered mobile + security questions
3. If identity confirmed, account unfrozen within 24 hours
4. Customer advised to change password and UPI PIN

### Customer-Requested Freeze
1. Customer goes to **Profile** → **Security** → **Freeze Account** to freeze
2. To unfreeze: call customer support or use app → **Profile** → **Security** → **Unfreeze Account**
3. OTP verification required to unfreeze

### Legal/Regulatory Freeze
- NovaPay cannot unfreeze until the legal order is modified or lifted
- Customer must approach the relevant authority (court, regulator) directly
- NovaPay legal team handles communication

## Notification Process

- Customers are notified via SMS and email when their account is frozen
- Reason for freeze is communicated (except in active fraud investigation cases)
- Customers are given clear instructions on how to resolve the freeze

## Suggested Response for Agents

> "Your account has been frozen due to [reason]. To unfreeze it, you need to [action required]. Once completed, your account will be reactivated within [timeframe]. I'm happy to guide you through the steps right now if you'd like."

## Escalation Criteria

- Account frozen for more than 5 days without resolution → escalate to Compliance team
- Customer unable to complete KYC due to document issues → escalate to KYC team for manual review
- Legal freeze → do not discuss details, transfer to Legal team immediately
- Customer reports they did not trigger the freeze → treat as potential fraud, escalate to Security team
