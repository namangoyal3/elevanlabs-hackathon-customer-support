# KYC Verification Guide

## Overview
KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is mandatory for all NovaPay accounts. It enables higher transaction limits, loan eligibility, and full wallet access. KYC is governed by RBI guidelines and must be completed within 30 days of account creation.

## Accepted Documents

### Identity Proof (any one)
- **Aadhaar Card** (preferred — instant e-KYC available)
- **PAN Card**
- **Passport**
- **Voter ID**
- **Driving License**

### Address Proof (any one)
- Aadhaar Card (serves as both identity + address proof)
- Passport
- Utility bill (electricity/gas/water) — not older than 3 months
- Bank statement — not older than 3 months

## KYC Process

### Option 1 — e-KYC (Instant, recommended)
1. Customer opens NovaPay app → **Profile** → **Complete KYC**
2. Select **Aadhaar-based e-KYC**
3. Enter Aadhaar number → receive OTP on registered mobile
4. Enter OTP → KYC approved instantly
5. Limits upgraded immediately

### Option 2 — Document Upload
1. Customer uploads clear photos of both sides of chosen document
2. Selfie with document is required
3. Review takes **2–5 business days**
4. Customer notified via SMS and app notification

## KYC Limits

| Status | Daily UPI Limit | Wallet Balance | Loan Eligibility |
|--------|----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| No KYC | ₹10,000 | ₹10,000 | Not eligible |
| KYC Pending | ₹10,000 | ₹10,000 | Not eligible |
| KYC Complete | ₹1,00,000 | ₹1,00,000 | Eligible |

## Common Rejection Reasons

1. **Blurry or unclear document photo** — Ask customer to retake in good lighting
2. **Name mismatch** — Name on document must exactly match name on account
3. **Expired document** — Document must be valid and not expired
4. **Partial document visible** — Full document must be visible in the photo
5. **Selfie mismatch** — Selfie must clearly match the photo on the document

## Re-KYC Triggers

NovaPay requires customers to re-complete KYC in these situations:
- KYC documents expire
- Customer changes their registered mobile number
- RBI compliance audit flags the account
- Account is inactive for more than 12 months and then reactivated

Re-KYC notifications are sent 30 days, 15 days, and 3 days before the deadline.

## Suggested Response for Agents

> "Your KYC documents are currently under review. The process takes 2–5 business days. You will receive an SMS once approved. If your submission was rejected, the reason will be mentioned in the rejection notification — you can re-submit corrected documents directly from the app."

## Escalation Criteria

- KYC pending for more than 7 business days → escalate to KYC team
- Customer submitted documents but received no confirmation email → check submission status in admin panel
- Document rejected 3+ times → manual review by KYC team required
