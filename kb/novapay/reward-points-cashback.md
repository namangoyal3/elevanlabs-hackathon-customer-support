# Reward Points & Cashback

## Overview
NovaPay rewards customers for every transaction made through the app. Points and cashback are credited automatically and can be redeemed for bill payments, wallet top-ups, or merchandise via the NovaPay rewards store.

## Earn Rates

| Transaction Type | Reward |
|-----------------|--------|
| UPI payments | 1.5 NovaCash per ₹100 spent |
| NovaPay Wallet payments | 2 NovaCash per ₹100 spent |
| Bill payments (utilities) | 1 NovaCash per ₹100 |
| Recharges (mobile/DTH) | 1 NovaCash per ₹100 |
| Loan EMI payments | 0.5 NovaCash per ₹100 |
| First transaction of the day | Bonus 10 NovaCash |

*1 NovaCash = ₹0.25 in redemption value*

## Cashback Offers

- Cashback offers are time-limited and merchant-specific
- Available under **Offers** tab in the app
- Cashback is credited within **48 hours** of qualifying transaction
- Maximum cashback per offer is capped (shown on offer details)

## Points Expiry

- NovaCash points expire **12 months** from the date they were earned
- Points are consumed on a **first-in, first-out (FIFO)** basis during redemption
- Customers receive expiry reminder notifications 30 days before points expire
- Expired points cannot be reinstated

## How to Redeem Points

### Bill Payments & Recharges
1. Open NovaPay app → **Pay Bills** or **Recharge**
2. Enter bill details and amount
3. At payment screen, toggle **Use NovaCash**
4. Select amount to redeem (up to full bill amount)
5. Confirm payment

### Wallet Top-Up via Points
1. Open NovaPay app → **Wallet** → **Redeem NovaCash**
2. Enter number of points to redeem
3. Points converted to wallet balance instantly
4. Minimum redemption: 100 NovaCash (= ₹25)

### NovaPay Rewards Store
1. Open app → **Rewards** → **Store**
2. Browse vouchers, merchandise, and experiences
3. Select item and confirm with NovaCash balance
4. Digital vouchers delivered instantly; physical items in 5–7 business days

## Points Balance & History

- View balance: **Profile** → **NovaCash** → **My Points**
- View history: Shows earn and redeem transactions with dates
- Points summary sent monthly via email

## Common Questions

**Q: Why haven't my cashback points been credited yet?**
> Cashback from offers can take up to 48 hours to appear. If it's been more than 48 hours and the transaction qualifies for the offer, raise a ticket with the transaction ID and offer name.

**Q: My points expired — can I get them back?**
> Unfortunately, expired NovaCash points cannot be reinstated per our terms. We recommend setting up expiry reminders in your notification settings to avoid this in the future.

**Q: Can I transfer my NovaCash to another account?**
> NovaCash points are non-transferable and linked to your individual NovaPay account only.

## Suggested Response for Agents

> "You currently have [X] NovaCash points worth ₹[value]. You can use them to pay bills, top up your wallet, or shop in our rewards store. Points earned today will expire on [date+12months]. Would you like help redeeming them right now?"

## Escalation Criteria

- Points not credited after 5 business days → escalate to Rewards team
- Customer claims points were deducted without redemption → escalate to Fraud team
- Cashback offer not applied despite eligible transaction → escalate to Marketing/Offers team
