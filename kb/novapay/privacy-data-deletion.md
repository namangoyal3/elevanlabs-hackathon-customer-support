# Privacy & Data Deletion

## Overview
NovaPay is committed to protecting customer privacy in accordance with India's **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023**. Customers have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of their personal data.

## Data We Collect

| Category | Data Points |
|----------|------------|
| Identity | Name, date of birth, PAN, Aadhaar (masked) |
| Contact | Mobile number, email address, postal address |
| Financial | Bank account details, transaction history, credit score (read-only) |
| Device | Device ID, IP address, app usage data |
| KYC Documents | Photos of ID/address documents, selfie |

## Customer Rights Under DPDP Act

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Right to Access | Request a copy of all personal data held by NovaPay |
| Right to Correction | Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data |
| Right to Erasure | Request deletion of personal data |
| Right to Grievance Redressal | Raise complaints about data handling |
| Right to Nominate | Nominate someone to exercise rights on your behalf |

## Data Deletion Request Process

### Step 1 — Submit Request
- **Via App:** Profile → Privacy → Request Data Deletion
- **Via Email:** privacy@novapay.in with subject "Data Deletion Request"
- Customer must verify identity via OTP before submission is accepted

### Step 2 — Verification
- NovaPay verifies the request within **5 business days**
- Customer informed of any outstanding obligations (e.g., active loans, pending disputes)

### Step 3 — Processing
- If no obligations exist: data deleted within **30 days** of request confirmation
- If obligations exist: data retained until obligations are fulfilled, then deleted
- Customer notified via email when deletion is complete

### Step 4 — Confirmation
- Deletion confirmation sent via email
- NovaPay account is permanently closed
- This action is **irreversible**

## Data Retention Policy

Even after deletion, NovaPay retains certain data as required by law:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------|
| Transaction records | 5 years | RBI guidelines |
| KYC documents | 5 years | PMLA (Anti Money Laundering) |
| Tax-related data | 7 years | Income Tax Act |
| Fraud investigation data | Until case closure | Legal obligation |

## Data Portability

Customers can request a machine-readable copy of their data:
- Request via app or email
- Data provided in **JSON or CSV format** within **15 business days**
- Includes transaction history, profile data, and preferences

## Grievance Officer

For privacy-related complaints or escalations:
- **Name:** Data Protection Officer, NovaPay
- **Email:** dpo@novapay.in
- **Response SLA:** 10 business days
- **Escalation:** Customers may approach the Data Protection Board of India if unresolved

## Suggested Response for Agents

> "You have the right to request deletion of your NovaPay account and personal data under the DPDP Act. The process takes up to 30 days. Please note that if you have an active loan or open dispute, we'll need to resolve those first. Shall I guide you to submit the deletion request from your app?"

## Escalation Criteria

- Deletion not processed within 30 days → escalate to Data Protection team
- Customer reports unauthorized data sharing → escalate to DPO immediately
- Legal notice received regarding data → transfer to Legal team immediately
- Customer asks about specific data regulatory compliance → transfer to DPO team
