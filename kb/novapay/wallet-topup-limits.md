# Wallet Top-Up & Limits

## Overview
The NovaPay Wallet is a prepaid payment instrument regulated by RBI. Customers can top up their wallet using UPI, net banking, or debit/credit cards, and use it for payments, transfers, and bill payments.

## Wallet Limits

| KYC Status | Maximum Balance | Daily Top-Up Limit | Daily Spend Limit |
|------------|----------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| No KYC | ₹10,000 | ₹10,000 | ₹10,000 |
| KYC Complete | ₹1,00,000 | ₹1,00,000 | ₹1,00,000 |

- Monthly top-up limit: ₹2,00,000 (KYC complete)
- Single transaction limit: ₹50,000

## How to Top Up

### Via UPI
1. Open NovaPay app → **Wallet** → **Add Money**
2. Enter amount
3. Select **UPI** as payment method
4. Enter UPI ID or scan QR code
5. Authenticate with UPI PIN
6. Money reflects instantly

### Via Net Banking
1. Open NovaPay app → **Wallet** → **Add Money**
2. Select **Net Banking**
3. Choose your bank from the list
4. Login to your bank portal and confirm payment
5. Money reflects within **15–30 minutes**

### Via Debit / Credit Card
1. Open NovaPay app → **Wallet** → **Add Money**
2. Select **Card**
3. Enter card details
4. Complete OTP verification
5. Money reflects instantly

## UPI Linking Steps

1. Go to **Profile** → **Linked Accounts** → **Add UPI**
2. Enter your UPI ID (e.g., yourname@okaxis)
3. NovaPay sends a ₹1 verification transaction
4. Confirm the amount deducted to verify
5. UPI ID is now linked and ready to use

## OTP Troubleshooting

| Issue | Resolution |
|-------|-----------|
| OTP not received | Check network, wait 2 minutes, tap "Resend OTP" |
| OTP expired | OTPs are valid for 10 minutes — request a fresh one |
| Wrong OTP entered 3 times | Account locked for 30 minutes; retry after that |
| OTP coming on wrong number | Update registered mobile in Profile settings |

## Limit Increase Process

Customers who need limits beyond the standard KYC limits should:
1. Complete full video KYC (V-KYC) via app
2. Submit income proof (salary slip or ITR)
3. Request is reviewed within **3–5 business days**
4. Approved limits are communicated via SMS and email

*Note: RBI regulations cap prepaid wallet balance at ₹2,00,000 even with enhanced KYC.*

## Common Questions

**Q: I topped up but my wallet balance hasn't updated.**
> If you topped up via UPI or card, it should reflect instantly. Via net banking can take 15–30 minutes. If it's been more than 1 hour, check your bank statement to confirm if money was debited. If debited but not credited, raise a support ticket with the transaction reference number.

**Q: My top-up failed but money was deducted from my bank.**
> This is a failed transaction scenario. The amount will be auto-refunded within 3 business days. See the Failed Transaction Policy for more details.

## Suggested Response for Agents

> "Your wallet has a daily top-up limit of ₹1,00,000 with completed KYC. To add money, go to Wallet → Add Money and choose your preferred payment method. If you're facing an OTP issue, please try resending — OTPs expire after 10 minutes."

## Escalation Criteria

- Top-up amount debited from bank but not credited to wallet after 24 hours → escalate to Payments team
- Customer requests limit above ₹1,00,000 → guide to V-KYC process
- Repeated OTP failures after account unlock → escalate to Technical Support
