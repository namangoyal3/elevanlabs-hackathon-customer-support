# Wallet Top-Up & Limits

The NovaPay Wallet holds funds for low-friction spending — one-tap payments, bill payments, reward redemption, and in-app P2P transfers. It is **not** a bank account; balances do not earn interest.

## Daily and monthly limits

| Limit type | Limited-KYC | Full-KYC |
|------------|-------------|----------|
| Single top-up | ₹10,000 | ₹2,00,000 |
| Daily top-up total | ₹10,000 | ₹2,00,000 |
| **Daily spend limit** | ₹10,000 | **₹1,00,000** |
| Monthly wallet balance cap | ₹10,000 | ₹2,00,000 |
| Per-txn UPI send limit | ₹10,000 | ₹1,00,000 |

Full-KYC customers can upgrade the daily spend limit temporarily (up to ₹5 lakh for 24 hours) by completing a video verification — available in **Home → Settings → Limits → Request increase**.

## Linking a bank for UPI top-up

1. Open the NovaPay app → **Home → Wallet → Add money**
2. Select **UPI** as the top-up method
3. Choose the bank from the list of BBPS-enrolled banks (92 supported)
4. Enter the UPI PIN for the debit
5. The amount is credited instantly to the wallet and available for spend within 2 seconds

The wallet can also be topped up from a **linked debit card** (no credit card top-ups allowed), or via **IMPS / NEFT** by transferring to the user's unique wallet VPA shown in the app.

## OTP troubleshooting

If the UPI PIN screen does not appear after tapping "Add money":
1. Verify mobile data + good signal; the UPI switch times out after 45 seconds
2. Make sure the phone's SIM for the linked bank is **in the device** — UPI needs the SIM to send a silent SMS for handshake
3. Check that the bank is **not in maintenance window** (usually 11 PM – 1 AM on Sundays for many PSU banks)
4. Force-close and reopen the NovaPay app — do not clear app data as that re-triggers KYC

If the OTP never arrives:
- For a debit-card top-up, the OTP is sent by the issuing bank, not NovaPay. Wait 90 seconds before requesting a new one
- The OTP SMS is sent from the short code **AD-NVPAY** — ask customers to check their "Promotional" SMS folder on Android

## Limit increase process

To increase the ₹1L daily spend limit permanently:
1. Navigate to **Home → Settings → Limits**
2. Tap **"Request permanent increase"**
3. Upload a **salary slip or 3-month bank statement** showing net monthly income ≥ ₹50,000
4. Review takes **2 business days**; approved customers see the new limit live immediately after the decision email

Premium tier (> ₹5L annual spend) and enterprise tier customers have limit increases processed in 1 business day.
