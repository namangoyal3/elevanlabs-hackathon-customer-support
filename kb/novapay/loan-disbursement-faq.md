# Loan Disbursement FAQ

NovaPay offers instant personal loans between **₹10,000 and ₹5,00,000** with tenures from 6 to 36 months. Loans are originated through our NBFC partner and disbursed directly to the customer's primary linked bank account.

## Eligibility

A customer qualifies for a NovaPay personal loan if they meet all of:
- Age **21–58 years** at time of application
- **Full KYC** completed (see KYC Verification Guide)
- Active NovaPay account for at least **90 days**
- Minimum **NovaPay activity score of 600** (based on transaction frequency, wallet balance, and repayment history)
- Net monthly income **≥ ₹20,000** (self-declared; verified against salary credits if the bank is linked)

Customers can check pre-approved offers in **Home → Loans → Check my offers**.

## Disbursement timeline

| Step | Typical duration |
|------|------------------|
| Application submitted | — |
| Automated underwriting decision | 30 seconds |
| E-sign of loan agreement | 2–5 minutes (customer action) |
| Bank IFSC verification | 1–2 minutes |
| Disbursal triggered | immediate after e-sign |
| Money credited to bank | **24–48 hours** for same-bank, **48 hours max** for other banks |

If the loan has been approved but the money is not yet credited, the most common causes are:
1. **Customer has not completed e-sign** — reminder via SMS; loan auto-cancels after 72 hours of no e-sign
2. **Incorrect IFSC / account number on the linked bank** — customer is notified; the loan is re-triggered after the bank re-links
3. **Bank holiday or weekend** — NBFC settlement runs only on business days; disbursal moves to the next business day
4. **RBI settlement delay** — very rare; customer sees a banner in the app with ETA

## EMI schedule

- EMIs are debited on the **5th of every month** via NACH mandate on the linked bank
- First EMI falls in the month **after** disbursal (e.g. disbursed 14 March → first EMI 5 April)
- Interest is charged from the disbursal date (not the first EMI date)
- If the NACH debit fails, NovaPay retries on the 10th, 15th, and 20th of the same month; after that, a late fee of ₹500 + 2% per month on overdue applies

## Prepayment charges

Prepayment is allowed **in full or in part** from day 1. Charges depend on the customer's rate card:
- **Standard rate card:** 2% of the prepaid principal + applicable GST
- **Premium / enterprise rate card:** 0% — no prepayment charges

Customers can prepay via **Home → Loans → Active loan → Prepay**.

## Agent scripting notes

- If a customer asks "when will I get my loan?" after an approved application, always check the e-sign status first — this is the #1 cause of disbursal delays.
- Do **not** quote specific interest rates on a call — each customer's APR depends on their activity score and is shown in the app. Quoting wrong rates is a compliance risk.
