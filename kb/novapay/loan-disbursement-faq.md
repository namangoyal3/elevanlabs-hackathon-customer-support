# Loan Disbursement FAQ

## Overview
NovaPay offers instant personal loans ranging from ₹5,000 to ₹5,00,000 to eligible customers. Loans are disbursed directly to the customer's linked bank account or NovaPay wallet.

## Eligibility Criteria

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Age | 21–58 years |
| KYC Status | Must be complete |
| Credit Score | 650+ (CIBIL) |
| Employment | Salaried or self-employed with proof of income |
| NovaPay History | Active account for minimum 3 months |
| Existing Loans | No overdue NovaPay loans |

## Loan Application Process

1. Open NovaPay app → **Loans** → **Apply Now**
2. Select loan amount and tenure (3, 6, 12, 18, or 24 months)
3. Review interest rate and EMI breakdown
4. Submit KYC documents if not already verified
5. Await credit check — typically takes **15–30 minutes**
6. Accept loan agreement digitally
7. Funds disbursed to account

## Disbursement Timeline

- **Approved loans** are disbursed within **24–48 hours** of agreement signing
- **NovaPay wallet disbursement** is instant after approval
- **Bank account disbursement** takes 1–2 business days depending on the bank
- Disbursals are processed on business days only (Mon–Sat, excluding public holidays)

## Interest Rates

| Loan Amount | Annual Interest Rate |
|-------------|---------------------|
| ₹5,000–₹50,000 | 18–24% p.a. |
| ₹50,001–₹2,00,000 | 14–18% p.a. |
| ₹2,00,001–₹5,00,000 | 12–15% p.a. |

*Actual rate depends on credit score and repayment history.*

## EMI Schedule

- First EMI is due **30 days after disbursement**
- EMIs are auto-debited from the linked bank account on the due date
- Customers can view full EMI schedule in app → **Loans** → **My Loans** → **EMI Schedule**

## Prepayment & Foreclosure

- **Partial prepayment** is allowed after 3 EMIs have been paid
- **Foreclosure (full early repayment)** is allowed anytime after 3 months
- **Prepayment charge**: 2% of outstanding principal
- No prepayment charge after 12 months of loan tenure

## Common Questions

**Q: My loan was approved but I haven't received the money yet.**
> Disbursement takes 24–48 hours from the time you signed the agreement. Check the app under Loans → My Loans for the expected credit date. If it's been more than 48 hours, escalate to the loans team.

**Q: Can I increase my loan amount after approval?**
> No. Once approved, the loan amount is fixed. Customers must apply for a new loan after repaying at least 3 EMIs of the current loan.

**Q: What happens if my EMI payment fails?**
> A late payment fee of ₹500 or 2% of EMI amount (whichever is higher) is charged. Credit score impact begins after 30 days of non-payment.

## Suggested Response for Agents

> "Your loan of ₹[amount] was approved on [date]. Disbursement to your bank account typically takes 24–48 hours. You should see the amount credited by [date+2]. If not received by then, please share your loan reference ID and we'll check the status immediately."

## Escalation Criteria

- Disbursement not received after 48 hours → escalate to Loans team
- Customer disputes EMI deduction amount → escalate to Finance team
- Loan approval reversed unexpectedly → escalate to Credit team immediately
